Mission Statement

How we work

Our team combines collaboration, communication, and our different backgrounds. We aim to build an easy-to-use online restaurant ordering system.

We value clear communication, testing ideas early, and leaving every project better documented than we found it.

Team Rules

Shared expectations

Respond to messages and review requests within a reasonable time.
Split tasks fairly and keep commits small, clear, and meaningful.
Respect different ideas, then make decisions based on the project goals.
Document major changes so the next teammate can continue easily.
